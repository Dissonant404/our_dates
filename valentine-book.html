21/12/2025

So it was the 21st of December, 2025, and I had just returned to Bangladesh the night before. Luckily, she happened to have some work in school that day, so we could literally meet the very next day of my arrival. I couldn't wait to finally see her after 3 whole months of long-distance together. So, the next day came, and I arrived early to school. I thought, why not buy her a bouquet on our first date? So, I picked one up. I waited outside the school gate, and I suddenly noticed her crossing the street. I gasped out of excitement. As she was crossing, the song "Every Breath You Take" was replaying in my head. We finally made contact, and she hugged me tight. It was our first real hug, but it felt like it was our last ever. This was the first time I felt the warmth of her body, and it felt euphoric. She was wearing a light blue cardigan with a white layered scarf, and it looked effortlessly chic and cozy. So, we went to Hot Cake and ordered two coffees and a brownie. As usual, her order was always an Americano. We started taking a lot of pictures together and displayed a lot of affection as we deeply missed each other. When it was starting to get weird in public, we headed to Ajwah, which was right beside. There, we found a comfortable seat, and I showered her with gifts and a letter I bought from Hong Kong. She was so happy with them; it meant a lot. My eyes lit up seeing her smile reading the letter. Then, we took some more pictures, and that's how our first meet really went. It felt nice and fulfilling, as if the dread of missing her was finally gone.

11/01/2026

It was the 11th of January, the day after her birthday. She felt quite depressed yesterday, so I was willing to spend a decent day between us to cheer her up. We met at Shimanto Square. I loved the khaki cardigan she wore. Also, the dark shade of red lipstick she wore looked absolutely bold and beautiful on her. Now guess what we did at first. That's right, we went to a cafe and ordered coffee. That moment, I gave her the remaining gifts that I kept secret for her birthday. A divine moment came when I asked her to be my valentine while giving her the moon charm. She said YES. I was so joyous. It truly meant a lot that I could be with someone dearly special during that day (even though we will be long distance again but still). Then, we headed to Rifle Square, and she bought me wireless earbuds she was longing to buy me, as I was actually in need of one for our FaceTimes. It was such a thoughtful present from her; I felt like I didn't thank her enough that time. In return, I bought her wired earphones as her old ones were damaged. She was quite happy too. After that, we went somewhere we were obsessively waiting for: PHOTO BOOTH. We made the most of it and threw in as many cute poses as possible. The photo booth was the best part of the day. I carry those photos in my wallet so I can cherish them wherever I go. Then, we stopped by Shajgoj and were looking at makeup and cosmetics. I noticed she was liking a specific foundation brand. I didn't hesitate and immediately bought it for her along with a kajal pencil (as she ran out of kajal). I just love buying stuff for her; it makes me happy, okay? I bought a niacinamide for myself for treating my acne. After being ragebaited for like a solid 20 minutes for not coming here, her friends finally came. Ngl, I felt kind of awkward but tried to stick with her. We bought some fuchkas and ate. Then we talked for a while with her friends. By 7pm, we decided to part our ways as it was getting late. I'd say the day felt nice.

19/01/2026

So, on the 19th, she had work in school again; that meant another hangout for us. She came with a slick MAGA outfit. The best part about her fit was how she rolled her thin jacket into a scarf; she looked super hot. Anyways, we stopped by Hot Cake first. This was when she handed me a surprise: it was a customized love letter for me. The letter consisted of all types of themes, starting from Batman, 500 Days of Summer, etc., with some photos of us, a lot of aesthetic designs, and a touch of her cologne. I LOVED it. I LOVED it so much that I kept looking at it again and again. How can a gift be THIS close to the heart? I was about to tear up right then and there. My absolute favorite part of that letter was a section where she compiled all the songs that remind me of her and told me to listen to them whenever I miss her. The words in that letter are really loving; I read them whenever I feel a little depressed to make me feel a little better. The gift means a lot to me, which I will forever cherish. Okay, sorry it's getting too long, but I feel like I haven't really expressed how grateful I am. Anyways, we decided to just ball it and walk to Aarong. There, we just looked at stuff, really expensive stuff, and clothing. We both didn't have the aukat to buy anything, but we took some cute pics, though. Well, to make that trip somewhat fulfilling, we stopped by Grassroots and ordered an iced americano and an iced latte for us. We sat by the window, took a lot of pictures, and recorded ourselves just having a conversation about random stuff and our future. Then, we were planning to walk to Dhanmondi, but her legs were getting quite tired. So, we decided to take a rickshaw. Our rickshaw ride felt so serene and pleasurable. We talked about random stuff like in our FaceTimes, reminisced about places in Dhanmondi, and overall had a great time. Our last stop was Chef's Table, where we had lunch. As always, her boring food taste ordered salad with chicken, which can't even remove hunger as a snack. I ordered a Korean rice bowl and fed some to her so she doesn't starve afterwards. Oh, we ordered two cups of tea, and something embarrassing happened. I accidentally spilled the cup due to my carelessness. I felt so ashamed. But her kind soul didn't mind and tried to help. I called for the maid; later she paid the maid for the troubles. She was so graceful during the whole ordeal. She brushed off my clumsiness and turned it into an act of kindness. At the end, we decided to call it a day. Before leaving, she gave a kiss, and her soft, warm hand scratched and caressed the back of my neck. It felt like I was on Cloud 9. Anyways, we parted ways, taking our own rickshaw back home. Overall, out of all, this was the best hangout with her. Spending this whole day with her felt complete, ideal, peaceful, and fun. Most importantly, everything about that day felt right and in place. This is quite possibly my best day of the month.
